# 1. Introduction

Agriculture is one of the most important sectors of the economy. Farmers often face difficulties in identifying crop diseases at an early stage, which leads to reduced crop yield and financial loss.

AgriSense AI is an Artificial Intelligence based farming assistant that helps farmers detect crop diseases from crop images. The system also recommends suitable treatments, fertilizers, weather-based alerts, maintains disease history, and supports multilingual voice interaction.

The goal of this project is to make modern AI technology accessible to farmers in a simple and user-friendly way.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Farmers often lack immediate access to agricultural experts and struggle to identify crop diseases accurately. Delayed disease detection can reduce crop productivity and increase financial losses.

The proposed system aims to provide a smart AI-powered solution that enables farmers to detect diseases instantly using crop images and receive useful recommendations for treatment and prevention.

---

# 3. Objectives

- Detect crop diseases using AI.
- Identify disease names accurately.
- Recommend treatments.
- Suggest fertilizers.
- Provide weather-based alerts.
- Maintain disease history.
- Support voice assistance.
- Support multiple languages.
